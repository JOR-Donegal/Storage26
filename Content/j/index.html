Week 10, 11

At the very high end of storage solutions, we see Fibre Channel infrastructures. Where highest performance, reliability, security are required (and cost is not an issue!) We have Fibre Channel.

Over most of the last 20 years, Fibre Channel technology was used for high end systems only. 20 years ago, it was the basis for the high-end infrastructure at ATU Donegal. And every time we worked with contractors who would designing installations for banking, the insurance industry, and with very large multinationals, we saw this technology in use.

But it is very expensive, and supporting it is a specialized technical task. We don't readily find skills on the market with an understanding of Fibre Channel. For all these reasons, market share for Fibre Channel has never increased. Most organizations will happily use iSCSI SAN technology.

However, this module will be incomplete without us covering Fibre Channel. Initially this week, read through my theory notes.

Next week, in the final week of this course, we will run through an extensive demonstration on a working system. Unfortunately, I've never figured out how to build a virtual environment to demonstrate all of this. I still must use hardware to teach this.
